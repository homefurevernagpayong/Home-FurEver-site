About Us

The Project Home FurEver is a community-driven action that has the initiative to protect the vulnerable stray animals within the Nagpayong Community. In many urban areas, stray animals face a daily cycle of hunger, disease, neglect, cruelty, and danger without receiving any form of intervention and protection. This problem is a reflection of a national issue here in the Philippines; according to the Philippine Animal Welfare Society (2025), as of 2024, over 13 million stray animals have been struggling to survive on our streets, left to fend for themselves without basic necessities. Every stray animal will be given the chance to be adopted into a loving and permanent Home FurEver. This project will ensure that no animal will experience any harm, especially in terms of health, medication, and well-being. By fostering care and responsibility, it will perceive and treat its four-legged residents. Moreover, to make this a community-based effort, we invite residents to visit our shelter to witness our love and care for every stray fur and promote a second chance in their lives through local adaptation.
